About Us

Welcome To ELIM Capital

Elim Capital Holdings Inc. partners with companies, investors and governments to unlock project finance, arrange strategic transactions and source off-market opportunities. We act as trusted intermediaries matching needs to capital, buyers to sellers, and local opportunities to international partners while guiding clients through transaction structuring, due diligence and execution.

Our Vision

To stand as West Africa's premier brokerage bridging capital, commodities, and real estate with integrity, transparency, and growth-focused solutions. By applying project management discipline and orchestrating every moving part, we ensure that complex transactions flow seamlessly from vision to execution.

Our Mission

To drive sustainable growth by combining deep market expertise, innovative strategies, and a trusted network, structuring and executing transformative deals in funding, real estate, minerals, and petroleum that deliver long-term value.
