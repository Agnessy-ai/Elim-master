OUR PHILOSOPHY

Ikemba Investment Group is a Pan African firm strategically focused on Development, Architecture, Construction Management and Consulting towards real estate in Africa’s emerging economies. With offices in Monrovia, Liberia and Accra, Ghana, our firm’s core approach involves working with public and private partners to help identify, plan and execute unique real estate investment opportunities across the African continent. About Us

Welcome To ELIM

Ikemba Investment Group was founded by a partnership of African professionals with over 50 years on industry experience, impassioned with the important responsibility to provide meaningful contribution in developing Africa’s vast economies that will leave a lasting impression. As the continent positions for enormous future economic growth, IIG aims to be an active leader in supporting the real estate sector. Whether you’re looking to invest in one of our developments, hire us to design and manage your projects, or seeking consultation for market entry into one of our focus markets, we look forward to working with you in redefining what real estate looks like across the continent.

Services

Services

Real Estate Development

We develop high-quality, sustainable real estate projects that drive economic growth and enhance communities.

  • Mixed-Use Developments – Combining residential, commercial, and retail spaces for vibrant urban environments.
  • Residential Communities – Master-planned estates, luxury homes, and affordable housing solutions.
  • Commercial Developments – Office buildings, shopping centers, and hospitality projects.
  • Land Acquisition & Site Development – Identifying, evaluating, and preparing land for development.
  • Joint Venture & Public-Private Partnerships – Collaborating with investors and government entities.

Architecture

We create innovative, functional, and aesthetically pleasing designs that shape Africa’s urban future.

  • Concept & Schematic Design – Turning ideas into visual plans and feasibility studies.
  • Architectural Planning & Blueprints – Detailed designs tailored to local conditions.
  • 3D Rendering & Visualization – High-quality digital models and walkthroughs.
  • Sustainable & Smart Building Solutions – Eco-friendly designs with modern technology integration.
  • Interior & Space Planning – Optimizing layouts for maximum efficiency and comfort.
  • Regulatory & Compliance Advisory – Ensuring designs meet zoning laws and construction codes.

Consulting

Expert advisory services that help clients make informed real estate and investment decisions.

  • Market Data & Analytics – In-depth research on property trends, demand, and pricing..
  • Project Investment Analysis – Feasibility studies and ROI projections.
  • Construction & Project Supervision – Monitoring quality, costs, and timelines.
  • Property Valuation & Due Diligence – Assessing assets for purchase, sale, or development.
  • Development Advisory Services – Strategy formulation and business planning.
  • Risk Assessment & Mitigation – Identifying potential challenges and solutions.
